Get Directions

BY CAR OR TAXI

As the Singapore Botanic Gardens has several entrances, we advise diners to enter via Tyersall Avenue (same access to the National Orchid Garden, Tyersall Gate and the Learning Forest Foyer/Drop-Off Point) from Holland Road.

Tyersall Avenue has been re-routed and replaced with a 2-way road. ERP gantry parking is available at the Learning Forest Parking Lot, adjacent to the Learning Forest Foyer. (Coach parking lots are available for parking of cars from 6 pm onwards).

Parking Charges
7.00 am – 12.00 am: $0.02 per min
7.00 pm – 12.00 am: $2.00 maximum
*15 min grace period for gantry entry.

Alternative parking is available at the Cluny Gate and Tanglin Gate entrances. From there, it is an equally enjoyable walk of 10 to 15 minutes to Halia Restaurant.

BY BUS

Disembark at the bus stop nearest to Gleneagles Hospital.
SBS Transit 7, 105, 123, 174, 502
SMRT 75, 77, 106

BY FOOT

The nearest entrance is via Tanglin Gate. From there, The Halia is a scenic 15 min stroll towards the Ginger Garden past the Swan Lake.
